Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we receive and collect when you use the ITS College Dashboard application, and how we safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

To provide you with the core functionality of the application, we collect only the most basic and necessary information. Specifically, we collect:

  • Full Name
  • Course (e.g., B.Tech, BCA, MCA)
  • Section / Batch
  • Contact Number
  • Email Address

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used strictly to create and manage your personal dashboard, generate your digital ID card, and match your attendance records directly within the app.

No Data Sharing Guarantee

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone. Your data is used exclusively to facilitate your experience within the ITS College Dashboard. We do not provide your data to third-party marketing services, analytics brokers, or outside organizations.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official support channels.
